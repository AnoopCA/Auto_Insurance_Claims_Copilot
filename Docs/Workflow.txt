AUTO INSURANCE CLAIM PROCESSING WORKFLOW

1. FNOL INTAKE

Receive Email / Web Submission / Phone FNOL
↓
Extract:

* Policy Number
* Insured Name
* Loss Date
* Vehicle Information
* Contact Information
* Initial Loss Description
  ↓
  Policy Search

Policy Found?
├── No
│     ↓
│  Request Missing Information
│     ↓
│  Wait for Response
│     ↓
│  Reprocess Submission
│
└── Yes
↓
Create Claim
Generate Claim Number
Create Initial Claim Record

---

2. DOCUMENT INGESTION & CLAIM CORRESPONDENCE

Monitor Email Inbox
Monitor Claim Portal Uploads
Monitor External Integrations
↓
New Document Received?
↓
Classify Document Type

Examples:

* Police Report
* Accident Photos
* Repair Estimate
* Medical Bill
* Attorney Letter
* Witness Statement
* Vehicle Registration
* Driver License
* Supplemental Estimate
* Settlement Documents
  ↓
  Store Original File
  ↓
  Store Metadata
  ↓
  Append Document To Existing Claim
  ↓
  Extract Structured Information
  ↓
  Update Claim Timeline

Repeat Until Claim Closure

---

3. CLAIM TRIAGE & COMPLETENESS CHECK

Review Available Documents
↓
Determine Missing Requirements

Examples:

* Police Report Missing
* Photos Missing
* Repair Estimate Missing
* Statement Missing
  ↓
  Missing Information?

├── Yes
│     ↓
│  Generate Request To Insured / Claimant
│     ↓
│  Wait For Documents
│     ↓
│  Re-enter Document Processing
│
└── No
↓
Continue Investigation

---

4. CLAIM SUMMARY GENERATION

Generate Initial Claim Summary

Include:

* Loss Description
* Accident Facts
* Parties Involved
* Vehicles Involved
* Damage Summary
* Injury Information
* Supporting Documents
* Open Questions
* Recommended Next Actions

Store Summary In Claim Portal

---

5. COVERAGE VERIFICATION

Review:

* Policy Status
* Effective Dates
* Covered Vehicle
* Covered Driver
* Coverage Types
* Deductibles
* Policy Limits
* Exclusions

Coverage Available?

├── No
│     ↓
│  Coverage Denial Recommendation
│     ↓
│  Supervisor Review
│     ↓
│  Claim Closure
│
└── Yes
↓
Coverage Confirmed

---

6. LIABILITY INVESTIGATION

Analyze:

* Police Report
* Driver Statements
* Witness Statements
* Photos
* Vehicle Damage Patterns
* State Regulations

Determine:

* Liability Percentage
* Comparative Negligence
* Fault Rationale

Store Liability Assessment

---

7. FRAUD EVALUATION

Review:

* Claim History
* Prior Loss History
* Policy Changes Before Loss
* Duplicate Documents
* Suspicious Timing
* Inconsistent Statements
* Image Analysis Results
* External Fraud Databases

Fraud Indicators Detected?

├── High Risk
│     ↓
│  Refer To SIU (Special Investigation Unit)
│     ↓
│  Hold Settlement Recommendation
│
└── Low / Medium Risk
↓
Continue Processing

Store Fraud Risk Score

---

8. RESERVE RECOMMENDATION

Evaluate:

* Vehicle Damage
* Injury Exposure
* Liability Findings
* Historical Claim Data
* Litigation Potential

Recommend:

* Property Damage Reserve
* Bodily Injury Reserve
* Expense Reserve

Store Reserve Recommendation

Supervisor Approval If Required

---

9. DAMAGE EVALUATION

Review:

* Initial Repair Estimate
* Supplemental Estimates
* Total Loss Indicators
* Salvage Information
* Rental Exposure

Generate Damage Evaluation Summary

---

10. SETTLEMENT RECOMMENDATION

Evaluate:

* Coverage Findings
* Liability Findings
* Reserve Amounts
* Repair Costs
* Medical Costs
* Policy Limits
* Fraud Results

Generate Settlement Recommendation

Store Recommendation In Claim Portal

---

11. SUPERVISOR REVIEW

Review:

* Claim Summary
* Coverage Decision
* Liability Assessment
* Fraud Evaluation
* Reserve Recommendation
* Settlement Recommendation

Approved?

├── No
│     ↓
│  Return For Rework
│
└── Yes
↓
Authorize Payment

---

12. PAYMENT & CLAIM CLOSURE

Issue Payment
Update Claim Status
Generate Final Claim Summary

Final Summary Includes:

* Coverage Outcome
* Liability Outcome
* Fraud Outcome
* Payments Issued
* Reserve Changes
* Closure Reason

Archive Claim
Close Claim
